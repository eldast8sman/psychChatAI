Psychinsights is a platform that helps in managing and analysing the wellbeing of people.
The platform is designed to help in the management of mental health and wellbeing of people.
Psychinsights has two tiers: Free Trial and Premium, some features are disabled for free trial users. Free trial only lasts for 7 days. After 7 days, users will be required to subscribe to Premium to continue using the platform.

The sidebar menu consists of Home(The default Selected Menuitem), Explore, Blog My Resources, My Goals, My Journal, My Journey, My Profile
